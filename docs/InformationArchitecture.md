# InformationArchitecture.md

## Website Type

Single-page MVP landing page.

The goal is not to create a full corporate website, but to present a focused industrial-tech service direction.

## Primary Page

Homepage / Landing Page

## Recommended Structure

### 1. Hero

Purpose:
Create immediate impact and clearly explain the new positioning.

Content:
- Main headline
- Short supporting text
- CTA button
- Industrial video or image background

Main headline:
Field Engineering for Hard-to-Access Infrastructure

Suggested subheadline:
Rapid diagnostics, industrial inspections, and field engineering support for critical environments.

CTA:
Talk to an Engineer

---

### 2. Services

Purpose:
Show what the team can do without overloading the page.

Services:
- Industrial Inspection
- Remote Visual Inspection
- Confined Space Operations
- Emergency Diagnostics
- Field Engineering Support

---

### 3. Industries

Purpose:
Show where the service applies.

Industries:
- Manufacturing
- Energy
- Utilities
- Infrastructure
- HVAC
- Heavy Industry

---

### 4. Process

Purpose:
Make the service feel practical, clear, and operational.

Steps:
1. Assess
2. Deploy
3. Inspect
4. Report

---

### 5. About / Backed by GFE

Purpose:
Connect the new Getnetic positioning with GFE Solutions credibility.

Message:
Backed by real-world engineering experience from GFE Solutions.

---

### 6. Contact

Purpose:
Convert interest into a lead.

Elements:
- Contact form
- Email
- CTA message
- Optional phone number
