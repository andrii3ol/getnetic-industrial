# VisualDirection.md

## Overall Direction

Modern industrial-tech landing page.

The site should feel premium, technical, serious, and operational.

It should not look like a generic engineering company website.

## Mood

- Dark
- Industrial
- Minimal
- Cinematic
- Technical
- High-trust
- Field-proven

## Visual Keywords

- Steel
- Sparks
- Pipes
- Tunnels
- Machinery
- Confined spaces
- Inspection cameras
- Thermal imaging
- Industrial lighting
- Engineers in real environments

## Style References

- Current Getnetic website structure and modern feel
- GFE Solutions industrial credibility
- Flyability-style inspection technology feel
- DEKRA-style industrial trust
- Industrial field operations / tactical engineering atmosphere

## Colors

Preferred:
- Black
- Graphite
- Dark grey
- Steel grey
- White typography
- Orange/yellow industrial accents if needed

Avoid:
- Corporate blue gradients
- Soft SaaS gradients
- Generic startup colors

## Typography

- Clean
- Large
- Confident
- Minimal
- Strong headings
- Short text blocks

## Imagery

Use:
- Real industrial environments when available
- Temporary premium stock imagery for MVP
- Real GFE photos/videos later
- Dark industrial video background for hero if possible

Avoid:
- Generic office workers
- Smiling people in helmets from stock photos
- Overused Shutterstock industrial images
- Abstract AI visuals
- Fake futuristic graphics

## Animation

Keep animations minimal and premium.

Preferred:
- Smooth fades
- Slow parallax
- Subtle reveal animations
- Background video
- Light technical UI overlays if tasteful

Avoid:
- Excessive motion
- Cheap hover effects
- Random particles
- Overly “AI startup” effects
