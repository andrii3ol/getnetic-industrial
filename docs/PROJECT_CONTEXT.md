# PROJECT_CONTEXT.md

## Project

Getnetic Industrial MVP

## Status

Concept validation stage.

The current Getnetic website remains online and operational.

A new MVP version will be developed separately under a staging environment (e.g. new.getnetic.com) and presented to stakeholders before replacing the existing website.

## Purpose

Create a modern industrial-tech landing page for field engineering and technical operations services.

The website should position the company as a highly specialized engineering team capable of operating in difficult, hazardous, and hard-to-access industrial environments.

## Primary Headline

Field Engineering for Hard-to-Access Infrastructure

## Core Positioning

Rapid industrial diagnostics.

Industrial inspection.

Technical operations.

Field engineering support.

Critical environment response.

## Target Audience

* Manufacturing facilities
* Energy companies
* Utilities
* Infrastructure operators
* Industrial maintenance teams
* Facility managers
* Technical directors
* Plant operators

## Key Message

We go where others can't.

Access. Inspect. Diagnose.

Built for critical environments.

Field-proven diagnostics.

Real-world engineering operations.

## Services

* Industrial Inspection
* Remote Visual Inspection
* Confined Space Operations
* Emergency Diagnostics
* Field Engineering Support

## Visual Direction

Dark industrial.

Premium.

Minimalist.

Technical.

Operational.

High-end B2B.

## Visual References

Existing Getnetic website.

GFE Solutions website.

GFE Technical Operations page.

GFE Instagram content.

Industrial inspection companies such as Flyability and DEKRA.

## Design Principles

Avoid:

* Corporate blue gradients
* Generic stock office photography
* Startup buzzwords
* Long text blocks
* Overly technical jargon

Focus on:

* Atmosphere
* Engineering credibility
* Industrial environments
* Strong typography
* Visual storytelling

## MVP Goal

Present a compelling vision of a standalone industrial-tech service offering.

The MVP is intended to validate positioning, visual direction, and messaging before collecting final content and production assets.
